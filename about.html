Who I Am

Alex Rivera —
Visual Artist
& Creative Director

Hello! I'm Alex Rivera, a visual artist and creative director based in New York City. My work lives at the intersection of fine art and digital design — I create brand identities, motion graphics, and immersive digital experiences that challenge conventional aesthetics.

Over 8 years I've collaborated with startups, Fortune 500 brands, and independent artists worldwide. My process is rooted in research and storytelling, then brought to life through meticulous craft.

When I'm not pushing pixels, I'm exploring urban environments with a film camera, studying vintage poster design, or teaching a weekly online art workshop.

My Philosophy

What Drives My Work

✦

Intentional Craft

Every pixel, kerning decision, and colour choice is deliberate. Good design isn't decoration — it's communication.

◈

Story-First Thinking

I believe design without narrative is just decoration. Every project begins with understanding the human story it tells.

⬡

Endless Curiosity

Inspiration lives everywhere — in subway tiles, vintage ephemera, and organic textures. I never stop looking and learning.
